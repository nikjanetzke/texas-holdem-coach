# Product notes

## Audience

People who want to learn Texas Hold'em from scratch or improve beginner/intermediate decision making.

## Tone

Simple, clear, non-intimidating. Explain decisions in plain English.

## Important guardrails

- Educational game/trainer, not gambling software.
- No real-money wagering.
- Avoid promising winnings.
- Teach risk, probability, discipline, and bankroll caution.

## Possible first screen

"Learn Texas Hold'em by playing practice hands and getting instant coaching."

## First MVP

1. Show player hole cards and table position.
2. Show current betting round.
3. Let player choose fold / check / call / bet / raise where valid.
4. Give feedback after the choice.
5. Track simple score: good decision / risky / mistake.
